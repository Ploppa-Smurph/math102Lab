    Base b1b+0, b312     Base 10 (Common Logarithms)    Base e (Natural Logarithms)

    4.3 - Properties of Logarithms
    
    a. The Product Rule: log_b(MN) = log_b M + log_b N
    
    b. The Quotient Rule: log_b (M/N) = log_b M - log_b N
    
    c. The Power Rule: log_b M^p = p log_b M
    
    d. The Change-of-Base Property:
    
    The General Property           Introducing Common Log        Introducing Natural Logs
    
    log_b M=(log_a M/log_a b)       log_b M = (log M/Log b)          log_b M = (ln M/ln b)
